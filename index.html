About Blossom Wedding's Company

Blossom Wedding (previously known as BW) is a Malaysia wedding planner that specialize in wedding planner and consultation. Blossom Wedding promises the creativity, vibrancy and skilled proffesionals to bring your beautiful and absolutely memorable dream wedding to life. Blossom team will have actively planned weddings for couples of all races, religions as well as mixed marriages from different countries. Blossom team typically offers wedding packages that are customized to the needs, desires and budgets of customers. On our websites, we have a special packages available, including flower decoration, food packages, honeymoon packages and more.
